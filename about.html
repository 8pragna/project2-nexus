About Us

Our Place restaurant is located in RR Nagar, Bengaluru, one of the most happening places. We offer our customers continental foods with a lot of variety, and the customer can also specialize their dish depending on availability. We are happy to say that we offer one of the best services in the city, and we have pretty good customer ratings with a total of 4.5 stars.

Our restaurant was opened in 2004, and it has been 20 years with an amazing history. We can tell you that we have the best food serving because we see the numbers increasing, but we do not have any franchise because we believe in uniqueness.
